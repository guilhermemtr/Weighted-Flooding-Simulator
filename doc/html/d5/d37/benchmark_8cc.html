round_t get_maximum_latency ( std::vector< std::pair< bool, round_t > >  test_data)

Computes the maximum latency of successful tests from <parameter test_data>.

<parameter test_data> contains many series of runs, where each series is composed of <parameter runs> number of runs. This method computes, for each series, the maximum latency of the successful runs, and the outputs the greatest maximum among the various series of <parameter test_data>. In other words, this method outputs the greatest latency among all runs in <parameter test_data>. It is assumed that the length of <parameter test_data> vector is a multiple of <parameter runs>.

Parameters
test_datathe (possibly multiple) series of runs.
runsthe number of runs of each series.
Returns
the maximum latency among all runs.
